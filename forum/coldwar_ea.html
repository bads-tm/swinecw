Actually B.C. (Before Caveman), Stormregion was terrible with their lack of response to most of the postings on this forums. But Caveman has shown us how an active community manager can make a major difference in customer/developer relationship, and in my opinion, clearly demonstrates how important it is to have a corporate voice at least trying to answer questions.
